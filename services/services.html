Services

  • Eat-in service: Provide a comfortable and inviting environment for customers to enjoy their food on-site.

  • Takeaway service: Offer customers the option to order and take away their food and drinks to consume immediately or to enjoy later.

  • Delivery service: Partner with food delivery platforms to bring food offerings to customers in their homes or offices.

  • Catering service Provide catering services for special events and occasions, such as business meetings, birthday parties and weddings.

  • Outdoor seating: Provide a welcoming and relaxing outdoor seating area, equipped with comfortable furniture and shade, for customers to enjoy their food and drinks outdoors.

  • Wi-Fi and charging station: Provide free Wi-Fi and charging stations for customers to use while enjoying their food and drinks.

  • Book Swap: Set up a book swap corner where customers can exchange books they've finished reading with others.

  • Entertainment Events: Organize and host entertainment events, such as live music, trivia nights, and book clubs, to attract customers and build community.
